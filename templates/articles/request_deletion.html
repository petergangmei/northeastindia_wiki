Speedy Deletion

For articles that clearly violate policies (spam, copyright, vandalism). Reviewed immediately by administrators.

PROD

Proposed deletion with 7-day grace period. Good for uncontroversial deletions where author can address issues.

AfD

Articles for Deletion with community discussion. For borderline cases requiring community consensus.
